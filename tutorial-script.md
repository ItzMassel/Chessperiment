## Tour 1: Board Setup (board-editor page)
1. **Resize Your Board** — Drag the edge handles to adjust dimensions. (action)
2. **Place White Pieces** — Select a piece from the palette, click a square. (action)
3. **Switch to Black** — Toggle color selector in the bottom panel. (action)
4. **Place Black Pieces** — Position your black army. (action)

## Tour 2: Piece Design (piece-editor page)
5. **Name Your Piece** — Type a name like "Shadow Knight." (action)
6. **Design the White Version** — Paint pixel by pixel on the canvas. (action)
7. **Auto-Create the Black Version** — Click Invert to mirror the design. (action)
8. **Define Movement Rules** — Switch to the Moves tab. (navigation)

## Tour 3: Movement Programming (piece-editor, moves mode)
9. **Understanding Move Rules** — Conditions like diffX/diffY define movement. (info)
10. **Build a Knight Move** — Add absDiffX=1 AND absDiffY=2, set Jump. (action + solution)
11. **Unlock Advanced Logic** — Click Advanced Logic for triggers & effects. (navigation)
